Joana Brito

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Computer Science and Engineering at the Group of Artificial Intelligence for People and Society (GAIPS) lab, INESC-ID institute in Lisbon. I am conducting research in Digital Education, particularly in the creation of reliable tools (I've played around with serious games and social robots) to meet the educational needs of children, with a focus on children with neurodevelopmental disorders. My educational background in Biomedical Engineering, particularly in medical devices, signal processing and feature extraction, provided me with interdisciplinary competences that help me bring a fresh perspective to the table.

Work

My PhD research topic is digital education, particularly the creation of technological tools to meet the educational needs of children, with a focus on children with neurodevelopmental disorders (Autism Spectrum Disorder, ADHD) under the valuable supervision of Ana Paiva.

So far I have played around with social robots (like the cute Elmo you see in the picture above) and serious games (on my Github you can find some of the games I developed for user studies). For a detailed look at my current projects, check out my Projects page.
